About

Independent notes for practical builders

AI Dev Tool Stack publishes plain-English guides for solo developers and small teams choosing AI coding tools, domain registrars, static hosting, and launch workflows.

The site is intentionally narrow. It focuses on tool fit, setup tradeoffs, and upgrade timing instead of broad software rankings.

Editorial approach

  • We avoid fake rankings and unverified pricing claims.
  • We update articles when product positioning or public plan details change.
  • We separate editorial guidance from advertising and affiliate disclosures.
  • We prefer practical decision checklists over generic product descriptions.

Monetization

This site may use display advertising or affiliate links. Sponsored or affiliate links do not change the price paid by readers. Readers should always verify current pricing and terms on the vendor's own site before buying.

AI Dev Tool Stack is an independent comparison site. Verify pricing and plan details on each vendor site before buying.
