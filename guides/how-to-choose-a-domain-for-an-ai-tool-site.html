Domain guide

How to choose a domain for an AI tool site

A good domain for an AI tool site should be easy to read, easy to remember, and broad enough to survive a change in content direction. Do not overpay for a premium domain before the site has traffic.

Affiliate disclosure: Domain registrar links may become affiliate links after approval.

Good domain traits

  • Two or three simple words.
  • No hyphens unless the unhyphenated version is impossible to read.
  • No trademarked product names.
  • Broad enough for future articles, not only one tool.
  • Renewal price that stays reasonable after the first year.

What to avoid

Avoid names that promise official status, copy a vendor brand, or sound like a support portal. Those names can confuse readers and make affiliate or advertising approval harder.

Practical recommendation

Start with the GitHub Pages URL until the site has a content direction. Buy a custom domain after at least ten useful pages are published and indexed.
