Launch stack

Domain and hosting stack for AI side projects

Most AI side projects do not need expensive hosting at the start. A static site, a clean domain, and a simple analytics setup are enough for comparison pages, waitlists, documentation, and affiliate content.

Affiliate disclosure: Domain and hosting links may become affiliate links after account approval.

Recommended order

  1. Build the first five useful pages locally.
  2. Deploy the site to a free static host.
  3. Buy a domain only when the site has a clear topic.
  4. Add analytics and Google Search Console.
  5. Apply for affiliate programs after the site looks complete.

Domain buying notes

Namecheap publicly describes its affiliate program as paying commissions for new customers after they click an affiliate link and make a first purchase. For readers, the practical buying criteria are simpler: renewal price, DNS controls, privacy, and support history.

Do not spend more than the site can justify. Content and search intent come before tools.
